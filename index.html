About

I graduated from the Bren School of Environmental Science & Management in June 2018 with a specialization in Coastal and Marine Management. Over the last two years, I’ve worked on various data science and client-based projects in fisheries, aquaculture, and ocean health. Currently working on a dashboard to visualize CA aquaculture data. Feel free to reach out with any questions, my contact information is at the bottom of this page!

Featured Work

California Aquaculture.

Created a CA Aquaculture Dashboard for synthesizing and visualizing existing data from a variety of sources, including US Department of Agriculture and NOAA Fisheries Statistics.

Explore Mariculture Data.

The Data Exploration Dashboard I created for the OHI Project provides a quick glance into the data behind calculating global scores. Read about it in my featured article!

Ocean Health Index.

Calculated status of fisheries, mariculture, MPAs, and social progress for the 2018 OHI Global Assessment at NCEAS. Synthesized large datasets and communicated research.

Seaweed Feasibility.

Investigated the feasibility of seaweed aquaculture in southern California for Santa Barbara Mariculture and the Sustainable Aquaculture Research Center.

SeaSketch for CGC.

Streamlined marine spatial planning for the CA Groundfish Collective and TNC by implementing SeaSketch as a cloud-based, planning tool.
