Seaweed Profitability

View the 5-year net present value of your edible seaweed of interest. Specify the current market price and whether you are interested in looking at an onshore or offshore cultivation system.

Seaweed Profitability Model

This interactive economic model dashboard was a joint effort by Ian Ladner, Shelby Oliver, and I. It supplements results from the economic portion of our master’s group project. Results include a 5-year net present value and annual profit break-down graph that was modelled using best available data compiled from literature. See final report for more information on sources.

Actual forecasting of seaweed yield and economic feasibility requires adjustment of original input parameters, such as actual seaweed growth rate or operational costs.
